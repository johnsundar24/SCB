8:15 am – 5:10 pm Washington, D.C. 



The IIF's fifth annual Washington Policy Summit was held via live-stream on Thursday, March 25. With the new Biden administration in place, this year's event provided IIF members with the opportunity to hear from government officials, leading policy experts and financial industry leaders as they discussed U.S. economic policy priorities and their potential implications for both the United States and the global economy. 

For questions or more information about the summit, please contact  meetings@iif.com
