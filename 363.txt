Transatlantic Relations and the Indo-Pacific: Implications for Japan and Korea
 Thursday, March 18, 2021 10:15 am - 11:15 am Please join us on March 18 for a special discussion on transatlantic security cooperation and security dynamics in the Indo-Pacific region.
CSIS and the Centre for Security, Diplomacy and Strategy (CSDS) of the Brussels School of Governance will convene a panel of experts to discuss security issues across the Asia-Pacific region and the importance of identifying areas of convergence, divergence, and cooperation among Asia, Europe, and the United States.
WELCOMING REMARKS
Victor Cha, Senior Vice President and Korea Chair, CSIS; Vice Dean for Faculty and Graduate Affairs and D.S. Song-KF Professor of Government, Georgetown University; Former National Security Council
KEYNOTE ADDRESS & MODERATED DISCUSSION
Moderator:
Victor Cha, Senior Vice President and Korea Chair, CSIS; Vice Dean for Faculty and Graduate Affairs and D.S. Song-KF Professor of Government, Georgetown University; Former National Security Council
Keynote speakers:
Michael J. Green, Senior Vice President for Asia and Japan Chair, CSIS; Director of Asian Studies, Georgetown University; Former National Security Council
Gunnar Wiegand, Managing Director for Asia and the Pacific, European External Action Service
EXPERT PANEL DISCUSSION
Moderator:
Michael Reiterer, Distinguished Professor, CSDS, Brussels School of Governance; Former Ambassador of the European Union to the Republic of Korea
Panelists:
Michael J. Green, Senior Vice President for Asia and Japan Chair, CSIS; Director of Asian Studies, Georgetown University; Former National Security Council
Ramon Pacheco Pardo, KF-VUB Korea Chair, CSDS, Brussels School of Governance; Reader in International Relations, King's College London
Eva Pejsova, Senior Japan Fellow, CSDS, Brussels School of Governance; Associate Fellow, Fondation pour la Recherche Strategique (FRS); Former Senior Analyst, EU Institute for Security Studies
Luis Simon, Director and Research Professor, CSDS, Brussels School of Governance; Director of the Brussels Office and Senior Analyst, Elcano Royal Institute
Sue Mi Terry, Senior Fellow, CSIS Korea Chair; Former Senior Analyst, Central Intelligence Agency; Former National Security Council
This event is co-organized with the Centre for Security, Diplomacy and Strategy (CSDS) of the Brussels School of Governance. This event is made possible by general support to the Center for Strategic and International Studies.
