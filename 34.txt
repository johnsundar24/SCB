How to advance WTO discussions on reinforced disciplines relating to the role of the state in the economy?


This roundtable focuses on disciplines relating to the role of the state in the economy.







              Research event
                          

13 January 2021 — 2:00PM TO 4:00PM

Online















This roundtable focuses on disciplines relating to the role of the state in the economy – with the aim to advance transatlantic cooperation on World Trade Organization reform.
The discussion will be held under the Chatham House Rule.
