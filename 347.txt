IIF Talking Policy: Discussion with Michael Gibson, Federal Reserve Board

8:00 am – 9:00 am New York / Washington, D.C.
12:00 pm – 1:00 pm London
1:00 pm – 2:00 pm Brussels
4:00 pm – 5:00 pm Dubai
8:00 pm – 9:00 pm Singapore



We were delighted to host a conversation with Michael Gibson, Head of Supervision and Regulation Division of the Federal Reserve Board, to discuss the top regulatory policy priorities in 2021.
During this session, we heard about the main priorities in the policy and supervisory agenda for the Federal Reserve Board and Mr. Gibson’s views on key current issues, including: 

Covid-19 impact and policy response
Credit outlook for the US banking system
The evolving agenda on climate-related risks
Emerging Non-bank Financial Intermediation agenda
Digitalization and the policy response, and more…


Hosted by:
Andrés Portilla, Managing Director, Regulatory Affairs, IIF


 
For questions or more information about the event, please contact  dlydon-advisor@iif.com
